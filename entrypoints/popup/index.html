On mangadex.org / weebcentral.com, OCRs pages and translates them into your learning language — click words to look up & track them. Toggle per page with the 📖 button. For much better bubble detection (and Japanese), run the local OCR server (manga-translator/server) — znam uses it automatically when it's up.

On a Netflix watch page, click 🎙️ Transcribe audio and share this tab's audio when prompted (Firefox requires this manual step every session — znam can't skip it). znam then transcribes what's actually said instead of trusting Netflix's own subtitles. Cloud tier sends captured audio to OpenAI — likely against Netflix's terms of service; your choice, znam doesn't block it.
